Lasagna

Description

A taste and rich recipe from the Italian cuisine that makes sure to delight any one person

Sure to impress even the most pickiest of eaters!

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Oranges
  • Possibly pasta

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes and oranges
  2. Mush with pasta
  3. Put in oven at 220 C for 25 minutes
